About Us Page

The Granada Theatre's motivation is to give its patrons the best theater experience possible. The Granada has developed programs over the years to make movies come to life, making it a location where movies come to life. The Granada Theatre reinvents the experience by fusing the desire to make movies come to life with modern theater amenities that genuinely awaken the senses and improve the movie-going experience it offers
